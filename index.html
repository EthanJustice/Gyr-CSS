About

Gyr-CSS is a light, responsive, and comprehensive framework designed to be inserted into the head of your webpage, and then left alone and forgotten. Gyr-CSS automatically styles many common HTML elements with a simple, elegant theme. Just place the link in your head tag, and you're good to go!
